Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

DO’s:
- Always verify links and websites before clicking or entering any information
- Use strong and unique passwords for different accounts
- Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) for extra security
- Keep your mobile apps and system updated regularly
- Be cautious while receiving calls, messages, or emails from unknown sources
- Use secure and private networks while accessing sensitive information
- Log out from accounts when using shared or public devices
- Regularly monitor your bank transactions and account activity

DON’Ts:
- Do not click on suspicious links or unknown attachments
- Do not share personal, banking, or OTP details with anyone
- Do not trust offers that seem too good to be true
- Do not use public WiFi for financial transactions
- Do not download apps from untrusted sources
- Do not ignore unusual account activity or alerts
- Do not save passwords on shared devices
- Do not respond to unknown calls asking for financial information

UPI / Financial Safety Tips:
- Never share your UPI PIN, OTP, or bank details with anyone
- Always check whether you are paying or receiving money before approving any request
- Do not approve unknown “collect requests” in UPI apps
- Use official and trusted payment apps only

Reporting Information:
- National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in
- Helpline Number: 1930


Reflection (100–150 words):

While researching about cybercrime and online frauds, I was surprised to see how easily people become victims of scams due to small mistakes. Many fraud messages and websites look very real, which makes it difficult to identify them, especially for students who frequently use online platforms and payment apps.

I learned that cybercriminals mainly take advantage of a person’s lack of awareness and urgency. Even a simple action like clicking on an unknown link or approving a payment request without checking can lead to serious financial loss.

After learning about these risks, I will be more careful while using the internet. I will always verify links, avoid suspicious messages, and never share my personal or financial information with anyone. This awareness will help me stay safe and responsible in the digital world.
